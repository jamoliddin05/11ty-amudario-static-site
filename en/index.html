OXUS WS AGROMETEOSTATION

The agrometeorological station designed for agriculture allows for the measurement of soil moisture, air temperature, humidity, wind speed, and direction. This information helps farmers make correct decisions during the process of crop cultivation.

JAYHUN TRAP PHEROMONE HOLDER

Pheromone traps are used in agriculture to control pests. It attracts insects using pheromones, allowing farmers to monitor the number of pests and protect the crop using fewer pesticides.

Oxus Airsense AIR MONITORING SYSTEM
