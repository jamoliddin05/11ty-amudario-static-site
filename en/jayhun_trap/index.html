Jayhun Trap PHEROMONE HOLDER

The modern pheromone trap is an innovative pest control tool that uses artificial pheromones to attract and trap pests. This environmentally friendly device helps to monitor the number of pests and warns about their spread in advance, which allows for effective control measures and crop protection

Pain points
Insect abundance

An increase in the number of pests can cause difficulties in their effective management

Infected plants

Pests inflict significant damage on crops and affect overall health

Performance and quality decline

Pest damage leads to a decrease in fruit quality and affects market value

INSECTS
Potato moth

It weakens potatoes and vegetable crops and hinders their growth

Plum fruit eater

The larvae enter the fruits and cause them to fall early

Comstock worm

A pest that sucks the juices of fruit plants and slows down their growth

Cherry fly

The pest's larvae decompose cherry fruits and cause their death

Apple moth

Their larvae make silky webs and eat and weaken the leaves of apple trees

Silkworm

Insect larvae eat large quantities of silk fruits and leaves of other plants and weaken them

How does it work?

The pheromone trap uses wide-angle mini-cameras to continuously upload high-quality images onto a cloud platform. These images are analyzed using artificial intelligence, and pests are automatically detected. The pheromone trap operates on a fully autonomous system, powered by solar energy. Communication is carried out through a GSM-mode.
